Purpose

The purpose of G-tubo.com is to provide an update and accurate weather report to living in Rivers State, Nigeria. The site will allow those who visit it to know the weather condition not only for that day, but also for a weekly forecast. Since outdoor activities are severely curtailed by heavy rain, snow and wind chill, forecasts can be used to plan activities around these events, and to plan ahead and survive them. Visiting this website you will have a pop-up on the weather condition

This webssite is designed for all age, both young, old, male and female who is interested in knowing the weather condition before traveling and planning an activity. The website is both laptop mobile phone friendly.
