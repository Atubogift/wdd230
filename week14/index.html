About Us

Together, with our business leaders and community partners, we will help lead the G-Tubo Chamber of Commerce to become the best place in the Nigeria for all people to live, work, and do business.The aim of our company is to provide new people with a chance to get into a business. In today's world with a generation of young entrepreneurs it's very easy to create a job for yourself. If you don't have the means to do what you truly want to in your day job then why not create a job that lets you learn new skills and do what you love. We hope to contribute to peoople outisde of our company. We hope to teach our new people new skills during their time with the company to get into business.
